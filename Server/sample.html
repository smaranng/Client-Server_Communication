All About Rabbits

Your guide to understanding these adorable creatures

Appearance

Rabbits are small mammals with fluffy fur, long ears, and a short, fluffy tail. They come in various colors and sizes.

Habitat

Rabbits are found worldwide and prefer environments like meadows, forests, grasslands, and even deserts.

Diet

Rabbits are herbivores and primarily eat grass, vegetables, and leafy plants.

Fun Fact

Rabbits have almost 360-degree vision and can see behind them without turning their heads!
